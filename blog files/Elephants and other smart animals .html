Why Elephants Never Forget — And Other Smart Animals

Some animals are not just cute — they're also incredibly intelligent! Here are a few brainy creatures that might just surprise you with how smart they are:

  • Elephants – Amazing memory, strong social bonds, and grief rituals.
  • Crows – Can use tools and recognize human faces.
  • Dolphins – Use names for each other and understand complex commands.
  • Octopuses – Escape artists that solve puzzles in lab experiments.
  • Pigs – Can play video games and have great long-term memory.

These animals show that intelligence isn’t limited to humans — the animal kingdom is full of clever creatures with impressive abilities.
