Ocean Habitat

Covering 70% of the earth, oceans are home to a vast world of marine life.

What is an Ocean?

Oceans are massive bodies of salt water thatsurrounds continents. They host a wide variety of ecosystems,from shallow coral reef to the deep dark sea. Ocean habitats support Whales, Dolphins, Sharks, Turtle sand million of fishes and pant species.

Types of Ocean Habitats

Coral Reefs--

colorful underwater structures formed by corals. Coral Reefs are rich in biodiversity, home to clown fish, sea turtles and anemones.

Open Ocean

This is the deep blue sea, far from shore. Whales, Dolphins, Tuna, and Sharks roam these vast waters

Deep Sea

Dark and cold, the deep sea is home to strange creatures like Anglerfish and Giant Squid. It's one of the least explored places on Earth.

Ocean Animals

Dolphine

Smart and social mammals that live in the pods. They communicate with clicks and whistles.

Sea Turtle

Graceful swimmers with hard shells, they nest on beaches and live most of their lives in the sea.

Sharks

Top preditors of the ocean, Sharks help keep marin life populations balanced.
