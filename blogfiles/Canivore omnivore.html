Carnivore, Herbivore, Omnivore: What's on the Menu?

Animals eat all kinds of things depending on their species. Let's take a look at how diets vary in the wild:

Carnivores 🥩

Meat eaters like lions, sharks, and eagles hunt or scavenge other animals for food.

Herbivores 🥕

Plant eaters such as giraffes, pandas, and cows live on leaves, fruits, and grass.

Omnivores 🥩🥬

Animals like bears, raccoons, and humans eat both plants and animals.

Fun Fact: Some animals change their diet depending on what’s available — even certain fish can shift between herbivore and omnivore!
