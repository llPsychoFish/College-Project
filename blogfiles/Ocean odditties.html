Ocean Oddities: Creatures of the Deep

The deep sea is one of the least explored parts of our planet — and it's full of bizarre creatures you've probably never seen before!

  • Anglerfish – Uses a glowing lure to attract prey in the dark depths.
  • Giant Squid – Rare and elusive, with eyes as big as dinner plates.
  • Goblin Shark – A “living fossil” with a long, flat snout and extendable jaw.
  • Vampire Squid – Despite its spooky name, it’s harmless and glows in the dark.
  • Barreleye Fish – Has a transparent head and eyes that point straight up!

The ocean is still full of mysteries — and these creatures remind us how strange and amazing life can be underwater.
