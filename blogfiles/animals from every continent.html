Animals From Every Continent: Who Lives Where?

Earth’s continents host unique creatures that have adapted to different climates and landscapes. Let’s explore who lives where:

  • North America: Bald eagle, bison, grizzly bear
  • South America: Sloth, jaguar, llama
  • Africa: Lion, elephant, giraffe
  • Europe: Red deer, Eurasian lynx, hedgehog
  • Asia: Panda, tiger, snow leopard
  • Australia: Kangaroo, koala, platypus
  • Antarctica: Emperor penguin, leopard seal

Every continent is a wildlife wonderland with its own set of animal champions!
